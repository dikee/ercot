World Cup 2014 - Group F

I'm been considering different scenarios to see if Nigeria advances to the next stage of the world cup. World Cup countries start off in 8 groups of 4 teams. The top two teams from each group advance to a win-or-go-home tournament to determine the winner of the world cup.

Points are awarded after each game played:

  • Win = 3 points
  • Draw = 1 point
  • Lose = 0 points
The advancing teams are decided by the following rules (excerpted and paraphrased from here):
  • Points earned in all matches
    • Ties determined by:
  • Goal difference in all matches (goals scored minus goals scored against you)
  • Goals scored in all matches
  • Points obtained in group matches between tied teams
  • Goal difference in group matches between tied teams
  • Goals scored in group matches between tied teams
  • Drawing by FIFA Organizing Committee

On June 22, 2014, Nigeria won a match (albeit controversially) against Bosnia-Herzegovina. Nigeria could make the tournament round of the World Cup for the first time since 1998.
